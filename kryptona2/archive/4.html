ESSENCE OF GLORY

Artistic and Marketing Director of ongoing game development project with Blue House Games

Blue House Games is a small game development company based in Ottawa. I joined the team the summer of 2014, and has designed the foundation of the Essence of Glory game universe based off of colleague Morgan Dawson's original works. Since then, the team has expanded; though I still oversee all concept designs for the project, my responsibilities now extends to human resources in coordinating the artists within my team as well as coordinating with composers, writers, coders, and marketing.

My strong fine arts background is to great advantage as I consider this game development project leaning more towards fine arts rather than technical on the spectrum. However, my current training as an industrial designer allows me to set realistic goals for the team and ensure marketability and feasibility of the product.

Blue House will release its first kickstarter summer of 2015.
