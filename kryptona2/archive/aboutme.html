Nice to meet you! I am a second year student at Carleton University, Ottawa, working towards a Bachelors in Industrial Design, minor in Physics. I am a curious and inquisitive mind that loves to explore, learn new things, and challenge myself to think outside the box. I believe my strong foundations both in art and science is one of my biggest strengths, and this proves to be a big influence in my designs. I believe that good design must be able to invoke emotional responses yet still not compromise practicality and functionality. I've had the privilege of working in a couple design positions, and my experience there further supports my pragmatic beliefs.

My interests in design includes UI design, software, electronics, and future technologies. For this reason, I am actively looking to expand my knowledge in these fields by working on personal software and hardware projects. Furthermore, I believe the integration of design and software development is a very exciting and much demanded field.

On my own free time, I still like to draw and play my violin. It is my way to relax so I can refresh my mind for the next set of challenge.
