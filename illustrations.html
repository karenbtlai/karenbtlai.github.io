Miscellaneous Pieces

(Image from Google)

Being a 2013 Visual Arts Arts Canterbury Program graduate, I am lucky to have a strong foundation in fine arts and illustration. I originally went through the so called "traditional" arts training, where I familiarized myself with traditional media such as pencil sketching, conte, watercolours, oil painting, etc. It was not until my last two years of high school where I took an interest in anime, where more emphasis is put on line drawings and digital rendering. From this, I familiarized myself with Adobe Suite, and was able to do freelance illustration and graphic design in high school. It is thanks to this that I have a keen eye for detail and aesthetics.
