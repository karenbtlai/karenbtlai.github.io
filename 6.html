MISCELLANEOUS ILLUSTRATIONS

A collection of miscellaneous artworks throughout the years

Being a 2013 Visual Arts Arts Canterbury Program graduate, I am lucky to have a strong foundation in fine arts and illustration. I originally went through the so called "traditional" arts training, where I familiarized myself with traditional media such as pencil sketching, conte, watercolours, oil painting, etc. It was not until my last two years of high school where I took an interest in anime, where more emphasis is put on line drawings and digital rendering. Coincidentally, this new drawing style and process is similar to the industrial design sketching style, which I am still perpetually exploring today. My newest experimentation is with 3D rendering.
