RED TIGER LABS - CLAW UI

And series of commercial works done as Industrial Designer and Marketing Consultant

Red Tiger Labs is a branch of Red Tiger Security and developer of CLAW - a program that visualizes data from Cyber Vulnerability Assessment on control systems, allowing ICS security teams to effortlessly locate the threat and help secure the network.

I was fortunate to be part of the Red Tiger Team as their industrial designer and marketing consultant from May 2014 to January 2015. During my position there, I re-branded the company image, and designed the first, second, and to be third generation of the CLAW UI, as well as the accompanying CLAW token.
