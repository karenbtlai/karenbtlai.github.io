@ deCODE'16 HACKATHON

I had the privilege to work with an awesome team of developers at the deCODE’16 Hackathon, sponsored by Wealthsimple. We were given the challenge to create a product to complement the current Wealthsimple long-term investing toolset. The final product is Travelsimple, a travel planning tool that estimates the cost of the trip according to destination, time of year, and travel style. One can then add the result to the Wealthsimple UI and start saving for that dream destination.

Who says personal finances are all work, no fun?

Personal finances doesn’t mean locking away every last penny in long-term investments. It means planning for the future while still living life to the fullest. Part of one’s monthly contributions can go towards long-term savings, while some short-term, to save for that dream trip. As the majority of Wealthsimple users are millenials, this tool will help satisfy their inner travel-bug.

1.
Pick your dream destination

Visualize your dream destination, the sky’s the limit.

2.
Plan the trip

Estimate how much the flight and hotels will cost depending on the time of year and trip duration.

3.
Select travel style

Are you the backpacker, general voyager, or the indulger?

4.
Save the date!

Travelsimple estimates the necessary monthly contributions to actualize the trip.

5.
Start contributing

The travel and contributions plan is added to the Wealthsimple UI. A portion of one’s original monthly contributions now goes towards the trip.
