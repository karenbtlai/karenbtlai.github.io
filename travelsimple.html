THE CHALLENGE:
Wealthsimple is a long-term automated investment tool set with its primary market being Millenials. However, there are generally challenges associated with millenials and long-term plans. Thus, the team was given the challenge to provide a short-term savings plan to append to the existing toolset. That is because we want people to know that investing isn't only about maximizing profit. People have lives and may have short-term financial goals accompanying their long-term financial goals.

The final product is Travelsimple, a travel planning tool that estimates the cost of the trip according to destination, time of year, and travel style. One can then add the result to the Wealthsimple UI and start saving for that dream destination.
