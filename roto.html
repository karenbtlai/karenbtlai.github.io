ULTRASONIC STEAMER ROTO

A two part research and product design project

A project done for the Fall, 2015 term at Carleton University's Industrial Design Program. This project consisted of 2 parts, the research portion and the product design portion. The research portion is a team task, where many aspects of the general steamer product is examined. This includes user values, target market, cultural probes, user cycle, existing product comparisons, ergonomics, materials, and mechanism. From this, a product opportunity is identified, and each team member branches off into their own individual product design based on their chosen market segment.
