PROBLEM STATEMENT:
Effectively monetizing a product has always been a vital problem space within tech companies alike. Unlike traditional retail where there is monetary exchange for physical goods or services, monetization are also made possible through in-app advertising or exchange for personal information. Instead of directly asking for payment for services or products, companies have been getting creative with their monetization methods, with the more prominent examples being:

  • Snapchat seamlessly incorporating sponsored adverts into "filters"
  • Facebook and Google selling users' personal information

Tinder's current revenue relies on its premium subscription, and only recently have they been experimenting with a sparse amount of ads. This invites an opportunity to brainstorm subtle methods in monetization through ads. Sublety is key, as an overcrowding of ads deter users.
